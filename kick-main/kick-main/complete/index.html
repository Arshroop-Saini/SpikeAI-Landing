SpikeAITM empowers high school students on their college journey through AI agent technology. We help students build strong profiles and craft standout applications to match with their best-fit colleges. Our platform simplifies the complex college admissions process, helping every student reach their full potential. Democratizing college admissions one spike at a time..."

Roadmap

Alpha Test

MVP development in progress, working prototype of the app ready for testing before the end of the year.

Beta test (B2B):

Collaborate with 2-5 local private and public high schools in the Miami area to test the MVP with students and counselors, gather feedback, and refine our product for production.

Gamma Test (BTC)

Allow students from anywhere in the world to use the product for free and provide feedback.

Launch

Launch the app to production for both high school counselors and students from around the world.
