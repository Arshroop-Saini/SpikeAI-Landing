Progress

Traction

So far, we have reached out to 11 facilities, all showing interest, and have been able to get Letters of Intents signed by two facilities. (Flick Football and CinQ Sports)

Financial Projections

With a projected $3 generated monthly per user in the first year, a $0.75 customer acquisition cost, and a high early app development cost, will we be operating at a loss in our early stages. As we gradually lower the development cost, and grow our user base, our projections indicate a breakeven in October, and 17k in profit by the end of the year.

Pricing

K!cK is a B to B to C, seeking to partner with facilities throughout the city. Our revenue comes from collecting a commission for each booking that takes place on the app. After multiple interviews, we have arrived at a fair price of 20% commission on weekdays before 6pm, and 5% on all other times.

Market

Launched in Bangkok, Thailand. Incubated through LaunchX with registered $250 of initial sales.
